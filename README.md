# panda-kitchen
Welcome to the **Panda kitchen!** its a high-speed 3D restaurant management game where you play as a master Panda chef serving the stealthiest (and hungriest) customers in the world: **Ninjas**.

## How to Play
* **Seat the Customers:** Ninjas will enter and find a seat.
* **Take Orders:** Walk up to a seated Ninja and press **[E]** when they are ready.
* **Prepare the Meal:** Head to the designated kitchen station (**Ramen**, **Sushi**, or **Roll**) and press **[E]**.
* **Serve & Collect:** Bring the dish to the table with **[E]** and collect your payment once they finish!
* **The Trash Can:** If a customer leaves before you can serve them, use the **Trash Can** to clear your hands of unwanted food.

## Controls

| Action | Key |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Move Panda** | WASD or Arrow Keys |
| **Interact / Cook / Serve** | **[E]** |
